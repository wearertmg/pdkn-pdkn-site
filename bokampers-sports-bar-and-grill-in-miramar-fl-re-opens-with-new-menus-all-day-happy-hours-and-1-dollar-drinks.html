Bokamper’s Sports Bar & Grill in Miramar, FL Re-Opens With New Menus, All Day Happy Hours, and $1 Drinks
Bokamper's Sports Bar & Grill - Miramar

Fort Lauderdale, FL - A staple of dining in Miramar, FL, Bokamper’s re-opens on September 20, 2022, with an all-new lineup, a new Chef and a brand-new menu in advance of a much-anticipated fall sports season, giving fans the opportunity to once again enjoy “The best game in town!”

Located along the water just off 75 S at 15500 SW 29th St in Miramar, this Bokamper’s location is known for its high-energy sports bar atmosphere, with both inside and outside seating, along with a private party room with its own bar and a dedicated kid’s game room, reopening under the direction of new Head Chef, Christian Zancolla and a new General Manager, Kirk Dreyfus. “We designed our menu to perfectly suit our guests and our Bokamper’s MVP cardholders,” says Chef Zancolla “This relaunch menu consists of brand-new items along with some popular Bokamper classics from over the years.”

One of the new fan favorites on the menu is the buffalo chicken tater kegs. Crispy jumbo tater tots stuffed with shredded chicken, buffalo sauce, Wisconsin cheddar cheese, cream cheese and ranch dressing. Perfect for game days or with friends and coworkers. Not to mention Bokamper’s award-winning savory BBQ pulled pork sandwich (guitar awards on the wall to prove it), a char-grilled churrasco steak with their very own house chimichurri sauce, a juicy steak & fries’ dinner, which includes a 12 oz New York strip steak topped with herb butter and crispy French fries. Can you smell that sizzle coming from the kitchen? Yep, the sizzling fajitas are back too! 

A sneak peek at some new items are the rigatoni & meatballs, a Mediterranean grilled salmon salad and a variety of new taco recipes - perfect for happy hour and game days! Items that have returned by popular demand are the fan favorite siracha-spiced tuna wontons, blackened chicken mac-n-cheese skillet, and of course, our award-winning smokehouse ribs.

In addition to these new and returning menu items, guests can choose from classics like Kim Bokamper’s signature burgers, sandwiches, healthy salads and our famous Killer B wings grilled in Bo’s signature “secret” sauce, and much, much, much more. Can’t forget the dessert options also available, like the baked-to-order cookie skillet and the ever-popular fried Oreos.

With “family” being a core value to Kim ‘Bo’ Bokamper, legendary Miami Dolphins player and an owner of Bokamper’s, he also offers a variety of specials and an all-day happy hour, along with game-day incentives! “Bokamper’s is a place to gather with friends, family, and teammates,” says Kim Bokamper. “It’s a place for celebrations, a place to relax after work and, of course, the best place to catch the game.”

On top of creating menus for the family, Chef Zancolla also prepared a special lunch menu, which runs Monday - Friday Open - 3pm and offers guests options of tacos, salads, sandwiches, and soup, all for $8.99 with the purchase of a beverage. And, as with all other Bokamper’s locations, Happy Hour is the finest hours, with two for one drinks all day, Open - 7pm, along with an awesome $7.99 bar bites' menu, which include items like the BBQ Pulled Pork Sliders, Mac & Cheese Balls, Chicken Tenders, Southwest Egg Rolls and so much more.

“When relaunching Bokamper’s in Miramar, it was important that we added more attention and detail to the fall sports season, because here at Bokamper’s we don’t just watch the game, we experience it,” says Emily Dall, Director of Operations at PDKN Restaurant Group. “We will always have the featured game of the day up on our giant TV wall and will be bringing back our weekday specials, which run Monday through Friday, offering our guests something different each day, like $5 burger night, Taco Tuesday starting at $3, Wing & Beer deals for Monday night football and on Saturdays & Sundays we have a Kill the Keg Challenge from 11:30am to 12:30pm with $1.00 16oz Bud Light draft beers.”

This season, Bokamper’s will also be re-launching their popular MVP Points Program and will continue to honor all collected points. To learn more about how to accumulate free points, free birthday treats and priority seating on game day, please visit the Bokamper’s MVP informational page here.

ABOUT PDKN RESTAURANT GROUP

PDKN Restaurant Group was founded by former Miami Dolphins player Kim Bokamper and his three partners, P.J. Kavanagh, Damon DeSantis, and Noel Cullen in 2008. Following Bokamper’s successful sports and broadcasting career he was approached by current partner Kavanagh about furthering his brand through a restaurant. The first Bokamper’s Sports Bar & Grill opened in 2008 in Plantation, Florida. 

Today PDKN owns three Bokamper’s Sports Bar and Grill locations in Miramar, Plantation, and off the Intracoastal waterways of Fort Lauderdale, Florida, all dedicated to providing exceptional service, great food, and an unparalleled atmosphere for friends, families, and sports fans of all ages. PDKN Restaurant Group also owns and operates Bo’s Beach, a casual beach restaurant featuring fresh seafood and stunning ocean views on Fort Lauderdale Beach; La Playa Dayclub, a Tulum-inspired open-air, house music day club located just steps away from the Atlantic Ocean shoreline in Fort Lauderdale, Bo’s Pub, a stylish sports bar located on the first floor at 1309 E. Las Olas Boulevard; The Balcony Bar, Fort Lauderdale’s only rooftop bar with a direct view of Las Olas, easily accessible on the second floor at 1309 E. Las Olas Blvd.; and PDKN’s latest creation The Hidden Garden, Fort Lauderdale’s newest upscale rooftop lounge and bar concept, located on the second floor at 1309 E. Las Olas Boulevard.
