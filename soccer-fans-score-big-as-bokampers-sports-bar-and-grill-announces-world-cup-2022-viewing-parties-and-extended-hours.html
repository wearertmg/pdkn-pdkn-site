Soccer fans score BIG as Bokamper’s Sports Bar & Grill Announces World Cup 2022 Viewing Parties and Extended Hours.
FIFA World Cup - Qatar 2022

Bokampers

Fort Lauderdale, FL - This year Bokamper’s Sports Bar and Grill opens its doors, and its hours, to host thousands of soccer fans for the most anticipated competition in the world, FIFA World Cup 2022. With three prime locations in Broward County (Fort Lauderdale, Plantation, and Miramar) and wall-to-wall HDTVs, Bokamper’s becomes the South Florida soccer headquarters! Featuring extended hours of operation to ensure great viewing experiences for all 10 AM, 11 AM, and 2 PM matches, except for those played on Thanksgiving Day.

“We are excited about the World Cup games,” says Kevin Sheahan, General Manager of Bokamper’s in Fort Lauderdale. “We love the energy, the excitement, and everyone coming together to cheer on their teams. We spent this past month preparing. We have tested hundreds of TVs, hired new staff, and created drink specials so that everyone can come and enjoy the World Cup experience right here at Bokamper’s.”

During the matches, Bokamper’s restaurants will serve its full food and drink menu and honor all daily specials. In addition, Bokamper’s will continue offering catering, its signature To-Go easy ordering option, and offering food service across UberEats and DoorDash for those wanting to watch the World Cup matches from the comfort of their own home.

With Google reporting searches on Youtube for this year’s tournament surpassing 2018 by 80%, it is clear that all nations and nationalities will be looking for a place to watch the games, and “Bokamper’s is the place to be,” says Emily Dall, Director of Operations at PDKN Restaurant Group, the parent company of Bokamper’s. “On our menu, it says, “Don’t Just Watch The Game, Experience It!” and we believe in that. We want people to come to our restaurants and be in the action of the games. This is what we strive for and what we will offer our guests during all games be it football, basketball, baseball, or soccer.”

ABOUT BOKAMPER'S

Bokamper’s Sports Bar & Grill was founded in 2008 in Plantation, FL. It has since grown to a total of three restaurants throughout South Florida. We are focused on being a local haven for fans of ALL sports. The unparalleled atmosphere makes this mega sports bar and grill different from any other. Televisions covering every wall, rival fans cheering their teams under one roof, and the overall excitement makes for the perfect experience for friends, families, and sports fans of all ages.

ABOUT PDKN RESTAURANT GROUP

PDKN Restaurant Group was founded by former Miami Dolphins player Kim Bokamper and his three partners, P.J. Kavanagh, Damon DeSantis, and Noel Cullen in 2008. Following Bokamper’s successful sports and broadcasting career he was approached by current partner Kavanagh about furthering his brand through a restaurant. The first Bokamper’s Sports Bar & Grill opened in 2008 in Plantation, Florida. 

Today PDKN owns three Bokamper’s Sports Bar and Grill locations in Miramar, Plantation, and off the Intracoastal waterways of Fort Lauderdale, Florida, all dedicated to providing exceptional service, great food, and an unparalleled atmosphere for friends, families, and sports fans of all ages. PDKN Restaurant Group also owns and operates Bo’s Beach, a casual beach restaurant featuring fresh seafood and stunning ocean views on Fort Lauderdale Beach; La Playa Dayclub, a Tulum-inspired open-air, house music day club located just steps away from the Atlantic Ocean shoreline in Fort Lauderdale, Bo’s Pub, a stylish sports bar located on the first floor at 1309 E. Las Olas Boulevard; The Balcony Bar, Fort Lauderdale’s only rooftop bar with a direct view of Las Olas, easily accessible on the second floor at 1309 E. Las Olas Blvd.; and PDKN’s latest creation The Hidden Garden, Fort Lauderdale’s newest upscale rooftop lounge and bar concept, located on the second floor at 1309 E. Las Olas Boulevard.
